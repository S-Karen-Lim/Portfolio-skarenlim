About Me

My full name is Shenghui Karen Lim, and along with my Chinese-Canadian birth, I have always had a curious mix of cultures growing up in and around home. Having these influences has allowed me to really appreciate going beyond stereotypes and standards, and being open to the differences people have is important to me. I put my best efforts into the things I know and also don't know (yet!), and I am someone who constantly craves improving myself in all areas of user experience design, visual design, and technical skill. I am passionate about creating real meaning and value for users through the the things I create, and I hope to be able to inspire others to do the same.
